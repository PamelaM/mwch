Shira Doron is a Franklin parent who brings us The Weekend Band. They play an eclectic blend of favorite songs in a folk rock style. They specialize in making small parties extraordinary with a fun mix of tunes you know and love. Partygoers of all ages from Worcester to Boston and beyond will want to sing along and dance to the music of The Weekend Band. You may even recognize them from a local block party or festival.

Check them out at www.TheWeekendBand.com.
